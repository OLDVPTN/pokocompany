Stages of work:

Stage 1

Planning

At this stage, the development team identifies the application's goals and needs, and determines the budget and schedule. This includes market research, competitor analysis, and understanding the target audience.

Stage 02

Design

Visual design and user experience (UI/UX) are created at this stage. This process involves creating wireframes, prototypes, and interface sketches to ensure the application is easy to use and attractive to users.

Stage 03

Development

At this stage, the development team starts writing code for the application, building functionality, and integrating various required systems and APIs. Development can be done in several phases: front-end (user interface) and back-end (server, database).

Stage 04

Testing

Testing is carried out to ensure the application is free from bugs and errors, and runs according to specifications. Testing may include functionality, performance, security, and testing on various devices.

Stage 05

Deployment

Once testing is complete, the application is ready to be launched on the market. At this stage, the app is published in an app store (such as Google Play or App Store) and can be accessed by users.

Stage 06

Maintenance

After launch, the app needs to be maintained by updating new versions, fixing bugs, and adding additional features according to user feedback. Maintenance also includes updates to maintain compatibility with the latest operating systems.
