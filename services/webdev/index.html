PokoCompany.

Empower Your Digital Presence

Welcome to the cornerstone of your digital transformation. Our web development services go beyond just creating websites; we design dynamic platforms that tell your story, engage your audience, and drive results in the ever evolving digital landscape.

We believe that a well-crafted website is more than a tool it's the heart of your online presence, the foundation for growth, and the bridge that connects you to your customers, fostering trust and loyalty at every click.

Stages of work:

Stage 1

Planning

The initial stage where the purpose of the website, target audience, required features, and content structure are determined.

This involves brainstorming, needs analysis, and creating a site map (page structure).

Stage 02

Design

Developing the visual aspects of the website (UI/UX).

This includes creating wireframes, mockups, and prototypes to ensure the design is appealing, user-friendly, and aligned with the brand identity.

Stage 03

Development

The coding phase where the website is built, including front-end (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and back-end (database and server-side coding).

Features like forms, APIs, or content management systems (CMS) are integrated.

Stage 04

Testing

Ensuring the website functions correctly, is free from bugs, and is compatible with different browsers and devices.

Tests cover performance, responsiveness, security, and user experience.

Stage 05

Deployment

The website is uploaded to the chosen server and domain, making it publicly accessible.

This stage involves hosting setup and basic SEO optimization.

Stage 06

Maintenance

After going live, the website requires regular updates to maintain performance, security, and relevance.

This includes software updates, bug fixes, and user data analysis.
